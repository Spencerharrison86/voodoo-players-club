The Vision & The Goal

The magic of beer pong is bringing people together — it fuels conversation, and keeps a crowd energized all night. The Voodoo Players Club exists to give that game the home it deserves, right here at The Voodoo Cellar.

This isn't about running a tournament. It's about building a community. A group of regulars who know each other, look out for each other, and show up every Thursday because this is their place. The kind of crowd that makes a bar legendary.

Section 02
League Structure

The Voodoo Players Club runs as a weekly Thursday night league with registered 2-person teams. Players build standings over the course of each month, culminating in a Monthly Championship on the final Thursday. League nights run on the 2nd, 3rd, 4th, and 5th Thursdays of each month.

Section 03
Community & Conduct

The Voodoo Players Club is built on a simple principle: the players own this community. When people have a team name, a ranking, and a reputation to protect, they show up differently. Regulars self-police because they have something to lose.

01
Respect is Non-Negotiable

Trash talk stays fun. Personal attacks, aggression toward staff or other players ends your night — and potentially your season.

02
Team Registration Required

No anonymous walk-ins. Players register with a team name before competing. You own your record.

03
Progressive Consequences

First warning, then removal for the night, then suspension from the league. Clear and consistent.

04
Good Vibes Points

An optional +1 bonus each night for standout sportsmanship — rewarding the behavior we want to see more of.

05
Staff Authority is Final

Bar staff always have the last word. The league supports — never undermines — the staff's ability to manage the space.

06
Fun Comes First

Competition is the vehicle, not the destination. This is a social league — community and atmosphere are the priority.
